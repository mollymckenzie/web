The US Census Bureau is the nation's leading provider of quality data about its people and economy. The Census Bureau conducts over 130 surveys each year, providing essential data that governments, businesses, and researchers rely on.

## Key Products

- **American Community Survey (ACS)**: Annual survey covering social, economic, housing, and demographic characteristics for communities across the US.
- **Decennial Census**: Count of every person in the US taken every 10 years.
- **Economic Census**: Measures economic activity in the US by industry and geography every five years.
- **Population Estimates Program**: Annual estimates of population and demographic components of change.

## Accessing the Data

Data is available through:
1. **data.census.gov**: Interactive tool for browsing tables and downloading data.
2. **Census API**: Programmatic access to hundreds of data tables.
3. **TIGER/Line Shapefiles**: Geographic boundary files for mapping.
4. **FTP Downloads**: Bulk downloads of survey microdata.

## Classroom Use

Census data is ideal for:
- Analyzing demographic trends in your local community
- Comparing socioeconomic indicators across regions
- Exploring racial and ethnic composition over time
- Housing cost-burden analysis
- Labor market and employment studies
