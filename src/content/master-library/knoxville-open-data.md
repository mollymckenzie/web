The City of Knoxville's Open Data Portal provides access to dozens of municipal datasets, updated on rolling schedules ranging from real-time to annual.

## Featured Datasets

- **KPD Incident Reports**: Knoxville Police Department calls for service and incident records, geocoded to block level.
- **Building Permits**: Residential and commercial permits issued since 2010, with permit type, value, and address.
- **Parks & Greenways**: GIS layers for park boundaries, trail networks, and recreation facilities.
- **Street Centerlines**: Road network with classification, surface type, and maintenance district.
- **Blight & Code Enforcement**: Properties with active or resolved code violations.

## Accessing the Data

All datasets are available for download at **knoxvilletn.gov/government/opendata** in CSV and GeoJSON formats. The Socrata API provides programmatic access for developers.

## Community Use

- Map code enforcement complaints by neighborhood to identify blight concentration.
- Track new building permit activity to understand where development is occurring.
- Analyze KPD incident data to understand public safety patterns.

## Classroom Use

- **GIS projects**: Use the parks and street layers in QGIS to analyze accessibility by neighborhood.
- **Data journalism**: Investigate permit trends across council districts.
- **Public safety analysis**: Are incident rates correlated with poverty indicators from census data?
