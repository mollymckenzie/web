The Tennessee Department of Education's annual Report Card provides school- and district-level data for every public school in the state, including Knox County's 88-school district.

## What's Included

- Enrollment counts by grade, race/ethnicity, and economically disadvantaged status
- Graduation and dropout rates
- TNReady proficiency scores by subject and grade band
- Chronic absenteeism rates
- Teacher experience and qualification metrics

## Accessing the Data

The interactive report card is at **tn.gov/education/data/report-card**. Bulk CSV downloads are available under the "Data Downloads" section. Knox County Schools also publishes supplementary reports at **knoxschools.org**.

## Community Use

- Compare proficiency trends before and after school boundary changes.
- Identify schools with high chronic absenteeism for community support targeting.
- Track demographic shifts in enrollment over the past decade.

## Classroom Use

- **Statistics**: Calculate and compare graduation rate confidence intervals across schools.
- **Equity analysis**: Visualize achievement gaps by income and race across KCS campuses.
- **Civic research**: How does per-pupil spending correlate with test scores in Knox County?
