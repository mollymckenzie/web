# Chicago Open Data Portal

The Chicago Open Data Portal provides public datasets for city departments and cross-sector community work.

## Highlights

- Public safety datasets
- Public health and social service indicators
- Transportation and mobility records
- Community-level civic service data

## Notes for Classroom and Community Use

This dataset collection can support teacher-led inquiry and policy discussions. Some themes, including opioid addiction and related public-health topics, are currently restricted to teacher-facing views in this demo.
