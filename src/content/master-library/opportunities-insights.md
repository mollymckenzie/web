Opportunity Insights produces and distributes high-quality data on economic opportunity and mobility in the United States.

## Key Datasets

### Mobility Report Cards
- Income mobility for children born between 1980–1991 at every college in the US
- Shows the percentage of students from bottom income quintile who reached the top quintile
- Geographic breakdowns at state, county, and commuting zone levels

### Chetty-Friedman Income Mobility Atlas
- County-level estimates of children's income rank given parents' income rank
- Allows exploration of the "Great Gatsby Curve" at the local level
- Interactive mapping tool available

### COVID Economic Tracker
- Real-time measures of economic activity (spending, employment, foot traffic) broken down by income quartile and geography
- Reveals how the pandemic differentially affected high- and low-income communities

## Teaching Applications

- **Inequality analysis**: How does the ZIP code you're born in affect your lifetime earnings?
- **Policy evaluation**: Do college graduation rates vary by family income at your local university?
- **Visualization projects**: Map mobility across US counties using the public microdata
- **Reproducibility**: All data comes with peer-reviewed papers describing methodology
